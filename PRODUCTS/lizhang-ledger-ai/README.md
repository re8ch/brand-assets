# 立账 / Ledger AI Finance Icon

Product icon assets for **立账**, a ledger-inspired AI finance system, designed in the RE8CH brand-asset language.

## Concept

The mark combines three ideas:

- a standing ledger book for `立账`;
- an upward financial line for money flow and portfolio growth;
- a yellow AI cross/star as the system intelligence core.

The visual style follows the RE8CH brand system: thick structural edges, transparent background, geometric color zones, and high-contrast SVG shapes.

## Files

- `SVG/icon.svg` - primary color icon with black edge.
- `SVG/icon-gray.svg` - luminance grayscale version.
- `SVG/icon-invert.svg` - inverted version for dark surfaces.
- `SVG/icon-no-edge.svg` - color icon without black edge.
- `PNG/` - rendered transparent PNG previews.
- `overview.png` - quick visual comparison.

## Naming

- Chinese product name: `立账`
- English product cue: `Ledger AI`
- Suggested usage: `立账 AI 理财系统`
